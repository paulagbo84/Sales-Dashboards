# 📊 Sales Performance Business Intelligence Dashboards

> A comprehensive Business Intelligence report analyzing 1,000 sales transactions across five analytical dimensions — built with R, Power BI, and interactive HTML dashboards.

## 📌 Executive Summary

This Business Intelligence Report presents a comprehensive analysis of the organization's sales operations across five analytical dimensions: **Product Performance**, **Profitability**, **Sales Performance**, **Regional Performance**, and **Time Trends**.

The analysis is based on **1,000 transactions** recorded between **January 2023 and June 2025**, covering:
- 7 Product Categories
- 5 Geographic Regions
- 6 Salespersons
- 4 Store Locations

## 🏆 Key Findings

### ✅ Top Performers
- **Best Product by Revenue:** Laptop — $502,896 (17.0% of total revenue)
- **Best Region by Revenue:** North — $611,797 (20.7% of total revenue)
- **Top Salesperson:** Bob — $541,159 revenue across 160 orders
- **Best Store Location:** Store D — $794,374 in revenue
- **Most Effective Promotion:** FREESHIP — $815,219 revenue generated

### ⚠️ Areas Requiring Attention
- Overall return rate of **24.8%** is critically high
- **Chair** has the highest return rate at **30.6%**
- Discount revenue loss of **$237,384** represents **8.0%** of gross revenue
- **Phone** category generates the lowest revenue at $320,421 despite 126 orders
- **October 2023** recorded an unusually low revenue of $45,071
